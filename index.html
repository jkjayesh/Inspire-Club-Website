Inspire Club

INSPIRE Club is a cultural and educational group. It is a nationwide network of doctors, engineers and entrepreneurs from prestigious colleges and universities of India. It trains modern youth in principle-centred living and leadership through drama, music, seminars, etc. Students can develop character with competence. We move with the motto 'Inquire Inspire Illuminate'.
It has a faculty of more than 120 doctors and engineers. It offers several systematic seminars on topic such as Time Management, Art of Mind Control, Positive Thinking etc. and courses on Personality Development, Public Speaking, Team Playing, Self Manager, Proactive Leader, Group Discussions, Character Build Up, etc.
It sends mentors all over India to many top ranking Institutions like IIT Kharagpur, IIT Mumbai, IIT Indore, BITS Pilani, NIT Surathkal, MANIT Bhopal, NIT Warangal, IT BHU Varanasi, SGSITS, M. S. University Baroda, etc.
It has over 30 branches connected to many of the leading Educational institutes.
